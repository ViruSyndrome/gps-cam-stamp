Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Summary

We collect nothing. No photos, no location data, no personal information is ever sent to our servers. All processing happens entirely in your browser.

Photos and Images

Photos you take or upload are processed entirely within your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. They are never uploaded to, stored on, or transmitted to any server operated by GPS Cam Stamp. The stamped photo is downloaded directly from your browser to your device.

Location Data

Your GPS coordinates are read from your device's Geolocation API and used only to generate the stamp on your photo. This data is held in browser memory only and is discarded when you close or refresh the page. We do not store, log, or transmit your location to our servers.

To convert your coordinates to a readable address, we use OpenStreetMap's Nominatim API. A request is sent to Nominatim's servers with your latitude and longitude to retrieve an address. This is subject to OpenStreetMap's privacy policy at osmfoundation.org.

Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics to measure page views and general traffic patterns (no personal data). We may display Google AdSense advertisements. Google's privacy policy governs how they use data from these services.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — address lookup (no personal data beyond lat/lng)
  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic statistics
  • Google AdSense — advertisements

Children's Privacy

GPS Cam Stamp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, use our contact page.
