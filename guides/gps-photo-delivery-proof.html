GPS Photo Proof of Delivery

📦 Delivery & Logistics 📖 4 min read 🆓 Free tool

Delivery disputes are one of the most common problems in e-commerce and logistics. A customer claims the package was never delivered. The driver says it was. Without verified photo evidence, it's one person's word against another. GPS-stamped delivery photos resolve this instantly.

Used by: Courier drivers, last-mile delivery teams, food delivery workers, e-commerce fulfillment teams, B2B goods delivery, furniture and appliance delivery.

Why GPS Stamps Strengthen Delivery Proof

  • Address on photo proves the package was left at the correct property
  • Timestamp proves the exact delivery time — critical for time-slot deliveries
  • Custom "Delivered at door" note removes ambiguity about where the package was left
  • GPS coordinates can be matched to the delivery address in your system
  • Dramatically reduces customer disputes and return processing costs

Recommended Stamp Fields for Delivery Photos

  • Address — confirms the delivery was at the correct location
  • Date & Time — exact delivery timestamp
  • Custom Note — "Delivered at front door", "Left with neighbour", "Delivered to reception"
  • GPS Coordinates — for rural or industrial deliveries where address may be imprecise

Workflow for Delivery Drivers

  1. Bookmark gpscamstamp.com on your work phone
  2. After leaving the package, open the tool — location is fetched automatically
  3. Take a photo showing the package at the delivery point
  4. Type your delivery note in the Custom Note field
  5. Use Classic template — full address and time visible at the bottom
  6. Download and share via your delivery app, email or WhatsApp to the dispatcher
