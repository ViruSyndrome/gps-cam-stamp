GPS-Stamped Travel Photos for Bloggers & Travellers

✈ Travel & Photography 📖 4 min read 🆓 Free tool

Every travel photo tells a story — but the most powerful travel photos also tell you exactly where and when that story happened. GPS-stamped travel photos add coordinates, place name and timestamp to your images so your memories are permanently recorded with their full context.

Why Travellers Love GPS Photo Stamps

  • Never forget where a photo was taken — even years later
  • Share photos that include the exact location — followers can find the spot themselves
  • Build a visual travel journal with location and date on every image
  • Great for travel blog posts — stamped photos look more authoritative
  • Impress friends and family with photos that show the full story
  • Prove you were really there — useful for visa applications and travel documentation

Recommended Template for Travel Photos

For travel and lifestyle photography, the Minimal template is ideal. It places a clean, unobtrusive location tag in the corner — your main subject stays in focus while the location is clearly visible. The Classic template works better for documentary-style travel posts where the location data is the point.

Suggested Stamp Fields for Travel

  • Address — shows the place name automatically (village, city, country)
  • Date & Time — timestamp your travel memories permanently
  • GPS Coordinates — for landscapes, mountains and remote locations where address may just show a country name
  • Altitude — great for mountain treks, high passes and viewpoints — shows how high you were
  • Custom Note — add the attraction or trail name: "Eiffel Tower, Paris", "Everest Base Camp"

Stamping Existing Travel Photos

Already back from a trip with hundreds of photos? Use the Upload tab to add location stamps to photos you already took. You can set the location manually by entering coordinates (find them from Google Maps for the place you visited) so every photo gets the right location stamp even if you're home now.

For Travel Bloggers: Instagram and Social Media

GPS-stamped photos are increasingly popular on Instagram and travel blogs because they give followers something extra — the exact location to visit themselves. The Minimal template keeps the photo beautiful while the location tag adds value for your audience.

Works Anywhere in the World

OpenStreetMap — the address lookup service we use — covers every country on earth. Whether you're photographing in Japan, Brazil, Kenya or Norway, the place name and address will appear in English on your stamp.
