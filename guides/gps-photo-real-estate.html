GPS-Stamped Photos for Real Estate Agents

🏠 Real Estate Professionals 📖 4 min read 🆓 Free tool

Real estate agents, property managers and home inspectors take hundreds of property photos every month. Adding a GPS stamp — showing the verified street address, date and coordinates — adds a layer of professionalism and verifiability that plain photos lack.

Used by: Real estate agents, property managers, home inspectors, rental property owners, commercial real estate brokers, mortgage surveyors.

Why GPS-Stamped Property Photos Matter

  • Confirms photos were taken at the listed property address — not another location
  • Timestamps prove when the inspection or listing shoot happened
  • Builds buyer and seller trust — verified photos stand out from unverified ones
  • Useful for pre-sale condition reports and post-sale dispute resolution
  • Required by some lenders and appraisers for property condition documentation

Recommended Stamp Fields for Property Photos

  • Address — auto-filled from GPS, shows the property street address
  • Date & Time — proves when the listing photos or inspection was conducted
  • Custom Note — add property reference number, "Pre-Sale Inspection", "Exterior — North Elevation", etc.
  • GPS Coordinates — optional but useful for rural or newly built properties where address lookup may be imprecise

How to Use GPS Cam Stamp for Property Visits

  1. Open gpscamstamp.com on your phone when you arrive at the property
  2. Allow location — the address should auto-populate within seconds outdoors
  3. Enable Address, Date/Time, and Custom Note
  4. In Custom Note, add the MLS number or a room label like "Master Bedroom" or "Kitchen"
  5. Use the Minimal template for a clean corner tag that doesn't distract from the property
  6. Upload the batch of photos you took to stamp them all at once

Pro Tip: Use the Minimal Template for Listing Photos

For MLS listing photos where you want the property to look its best, use the Minimal template — it places a small, unobtrusive location tag in the bottom-left corner. For inspection and condition report photos, use the Classic template for a full visible information bar.

Batch Stamping Multiple Property Photos

After a property shoot, upload all photos at once using the Upload tab. GPS Cam Stamp will stamp all of them with the same location and template. Download them as a batch — ideal for producing a complete, timestamped photo report.
