Common Issues

  • Camera not working — Make sure you've allowed camera permission in your browser. On iPhone, use Safari. On Android, use Chrome.
  • GPS not found — Allow location permission when prompted. Try outdoors for better accuracy. On desktop, browser GPS uses Wi-Fi triangulation and may be less accurate.
  • No address shown — Address lookup requires an internet connection. Coordinates will still be stamped if address lookup fails.
  • Photo not downloading — Try a different browser. Safari on iOS may require you to tap and hold the image to save.
