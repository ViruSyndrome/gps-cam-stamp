About GPS Cam Stamp

Built out of frustration with apps that charge $60/month for a simple feature that should be free.

Why We Built This

GPS camera apps on the App Store and Google Play have a combined 100+ million downloads — which tells you how much people need this feature. But almost every app buries it behind aggressive ads, confusing trials, or subscription plans costing up to $60/month.

GPS Cam Stamp was built to fix that. It does one thing extremely well: it stamps your photos with GPS coordinates, address, altitude and timestamp — instantly, in your browser, with no install, no account and no payment.

How It Works

Everything runs entirely in your browser using standard Web APIs:

  • Geolocation API — reads GPS coordinates directly from your device
  • MediaDevices API — accesses your camera for live capture
  • HTML5 Canvas API — draws the stamp onto your photo
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — converts coordinates to a readable street address (free, no API key)

Your photos are never uploaded to any server. The stamp is applied locally and the file is downloaded directly to your device.

Who Uses GPS Cam Stamp

Our users include field workers, construction site managers, real estate agents, insurance adjusters, delivery personnel, NGO workers, travel bloggers, remote freelancers and anyone who needs a verified, location-stamped photo quickly — from any device including a laptop.

Our Commitment
